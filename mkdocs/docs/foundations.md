# The Big Four of the Foundation

## Instrument
Normalized set of instruments sourced from exchanges and extended to support bilateral physical and financial deals

* Futures
* Swaps
* Options
* Physical Trades
* Load Forecasts (Power)

## Counterparty
Legal entity definitions as well as rollup capabilities

## Trade
Representations of executed trades using Instrument definitions

* Single Leg
* Multi Leg
* Net Positions
* Active Trades
* Historical Trades
* Lifecycle Activities

## Market Data
Market data from exchanges as well as derived curve management

* Real-time prices
* Settlement prices
* Historical prices
* Derived curves
* Weighted curves
* Foundational statistical calculations (stddev, variance)
